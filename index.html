Our confession

“Then you will know the truth, and the truth will set you free.”

John 8:32

We are a non-denominational Church of Christ, preaching one God, modesty, love, and true Christianity. The Bible is the authority of all our teaching, and the Holy Spirit is its revealer. We live watching for the return of Christ. We worship in every language, and our church family is growing strongly across East Africa.

What we do

Four ways we carry the truth

01

Swahili Worship

A spiritual home for the Swahili-speaking church in Iowa, gathered weekly for IBADA around Scripture, prayer, and song.

02

Media & Technology

Live broadcasts, music, and apps that put Scripture into people's hands far beyond our walls.

03

Compassion

Relief and dignity for the poor, the homeless, and the distressed in our own community.

04

Africa Missions

Walking alongside churches and ministries in Kenya, Tanzania, and the DR Congo as one family across the water.

Leadership

The servants who lead

Meshak Maliyabwana M.

President & Media

Widely known as Apostle Maliyabwana. The gospel set him free from drugs and alcohol in 2020, and he founded this ministry the same year. With the new life came talents he never trained for: video editing, music writing and production, and graphic design. He credits them to the Holy Spirit and pours them into the broadcast every week.

Selemani Bulako

Secretary · Overseer, USA & Europe

The first pastor of Ukweli Church of Christ in Iowa City, trained personally by Apostle Maliyabwana. He oversees our churches in the USA and our members across Europe, preaching, teaching, and doing outreach. He also helps non-English speakers in the community find jobs and get on their feet.

Barnaba Kabwe

Treasurer · Youth Representative

Leads our youth ministry, mentoring young men to overcome alcohol, drugs, and the other desires of youth.
